Our Audience

Our audience will be comprised mostly of those who run businesses and those who are trying to find businesses to work with or for.

Business Owners

The average business owners in our area either run a chain or have start-up companies. Both will be looking for advertising and connections in this highly competitive area. Most will be middle aged, and have some knowledge of common business practices. Perfect for our Commerce Community!

Job Seekers/Customers

Most of our audience will be either job seeking or simply trying to find a service provided by a business in our directory. These groups will have varying ages and skills, as well as beliefs and lifestyles. We can appeal to this broad group by applying helpful web design practices that guide our users where they want to go.

Scenarios
  • A new business owner wants to know how he can get his business listed in our directory.
  • An experienced multi franchise owner wants to know how much it will cost to put his companies in our advertisements.
  • A young father is looking for a nearby entry level job in the tech industry. Multiple tech businesses are listed on our site.
  • A high level business executive is intrigued by our networking strategies and wants to ask about it.
  • A stay at home mom needs a plumber asap. She looks at our directory to find one.
