Our Audience

Our audience will be comprised mostly of those who run businesses and those who are trying to find businesses to work with or for.

Kathy Wilman

Kathy is a 46 year old mother of 3 and a business owner. Her company produces art supplies here in St. George, and she supplies to a few schools in the area.

Kevin Gilman

Kevin is a 27 year old husband and father of 2 who is outgoing and driven. He's always looking for ways to improve is financial standings.

Scenarios
  • Kathy has recently heard of our site and she wants to know how she can get her company in our directory
  • Kathy sees on of our ads and wants to know how much it will cost to put her company in our advertisements.
  • Kevin has recently been schooling for a certificate in the technology area and he comes to our website to look for tech companies in St. George
  • After seeing more schools come to her business after one of our ads, Kathy is impressed. She wants to contact us to talk about our advertising strategies.
  • The piping under Kevin's kitchen sink has exploded. Kevin thinks he can fix it, but his wise wife insists they call a plumber. Kevin comes to out site to find one in town.
